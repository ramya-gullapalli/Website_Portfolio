About Me

I am an undergraduate student attending Georgia Institute of Technology as a Computational Media major. I hope to assert myself in the technology industry and use my previous work and volunteering experience to gain more opportunities within my field. I am keen on learning and going above and beyond as I hope to help with creating design technologies capable of improving the human condition.

TED Talk

During sophmore year of high school I wrote and presented a Ted Talk to a room full of 200+ people. Later the next year, I became editor of our club and helped others with their speeches in their Ted Talks.

Gallery App

For one of my CS classes I coded and created a gallery app that uses an Itunes Search API to provide images as a result of the search bar. Images can be found using a filter and can be shuffled by hitting the play button.

Infostretch Internship

During Summer of 2022 I took part in a remote data engineering internship where I worked with like-minded individuals on data storytelling and cleaning large datasets.
